Gross Margin & Price Pass-Through

How crude cost changes translate to retail prices · EU Bulletin pre-tax + EIA Brent + ECB EUR/USD · 2-year weekly

Gross margin = Ireland pre-tax retail price (EU Oil Bulletin depot price, ex all tax) minus Brent crude cost converted to €/litre — it captures refinery processing, transport, distribution, and dealer markup combined. Pass-through (β) is the fraction of a €1 move in Brent crude that appears in the pre-tax retail price within the same week. β = 1.0 means full, immediate pass-through. β < 1.0 means costs are partially absorbed. If β_rising > β_falling, retailers transmit cost increases faster than cost savings — the standard empirical signal of asymmetric pricing and potential market power. Note: EU Bulletin prices are survey depot prices; oilprices.ie tracks the higher final delivery price.
