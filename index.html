📊 Weekly Market Fundamentals

EIA Weekly Petroleum Status Report · updated every Thursday · current as of most recent report

The weekly inventory draw/build is the single most-watched number in oil markets — a surprise draw tends to push prices up; a surprise build pushes them down. Refinery utilization signals near-term crude demand; production shows the US supply response.
